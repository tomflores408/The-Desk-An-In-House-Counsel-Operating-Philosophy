# Removing Friction

One of the simplest but most powerful insights is this: salespeople are at their best when they are focused on selling. It is not possible to be a competent salesperson, a tough negotiator, and a skilled contract drafter in the same lifetime. 

We began a methodical elimination campaign:

- Contract negotiation? Handled by the legal team.
- Technical questions? Routed directly to engineers.
- Pricing structures? Pre-approved and documented.
- Follow-up requests? Handled by the legal team.

The result was immediate. When salespeople were allowed to spend 100% of their energy selling, they didn't just close more deals—they were happier doing it.

Sales and Marketing arts require **peace.** When salespeople are scared, upset, sad, or fatigued, they cannot sell or create. Indeed, the Italian Renaissance was only possible during the years of peace.

It is the duty of the Legal Team to ensure that creatives are protected from distraction. Attorneys are trained in handling disputes and administration. Problems that would give other people ulcers do not even register with us.

Look at your top performers. Calculate how many hours a week they spend on tasks that don't drive revenue. Give those tasks to other teams, and you unlock growth without hiring a single new salesperson.
