# Tell the Sales Team What You Need

It is important to show the Sales Team that you are on the same side. 

Explain how front loading the deal speeds up close time. Explain that they are in the best position to stop the buyer from inserting inapplicable terms into the contract by explaining the technology.

Tell them that you need every detail they have about this deal. Explain how most in-house lawyers have more than 10 years of transactional and litigation experience. It is extremely difficult to give an experienced lawyer more documents than they can handle. That's like trying to drown a fish. 200 pages is a lot to most people, but not to us. 

Alignment between sales and legal is not a luxury. It is the prerequisite for velocity.
