# Tell the Sales Team What You Need

We are on the same side.

Front load the deal. Explain the technology and data flow to the procurement officer as soon as possible to prevent them from inserting inapplicable terms into the contract.

Provide the Legal Team with every detail you have. Most in-house lawyers have more than 10 years of transactional and litigation experience. It is very difficult to give an experienced lawyer more documents than they can handle.

Answer your phone. Most in-house lawyers cover every salesperson in the company, and we live in our communications tools. When a deal is moving, we move with it.

Alignment between sales and legal is not a luxury. It is the prerequisite for velocity.
