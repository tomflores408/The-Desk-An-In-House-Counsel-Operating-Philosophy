# Call Preparation

Preparing for a court hearing is a lot like warming up for a fight: you rehearse your points to be as hard and effective as possible. This is a mistake in transaction calls.

I recommend calming down as much as possible. Listen to guided meditations and get your heart rate down as low as possible. The purpose of the call is to find an agreement. There is no judge to award victory here.

Come to the negotiation with a quiet mind.
