# Leave Litigation at the Door

Litigators are the stars of the courtroom. In transactions, the Account Executive is the lead singer, the Sales Engineer is the lead guitarist, and the attorney is the drummer. At most, the attorney will get a quick solo, but in no way should the attorney steal the spotlight from the Account Executive. Do not upstage the AE or the product.

Keep the show moving. Stay on point. Maintain composure.
