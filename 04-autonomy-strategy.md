# The Autonomy Strategy

In the early days, I touched every case. I reviewed every document. I was the safety net. But you cannot 3X a company if any one person is the bottleneck for every document.

I realized that if I wanted to scale, I had to give my staff attorneys true autonomy. This wasn't just about delegation; it was about ownership. I gave them full control over their cases. I stopped hovering.

But something amazing happened. By giving them autonomy, they took ownership. They stopped acting like employees and started acting like advocates. They grew faster, the internal clients were happier, and I was freed up to focus on the strategy of the firm rather than the minutiae of the files.

You can have control, or you can have growth. You rarely get to keep both. Trust your team to do the job you hired them to do.
